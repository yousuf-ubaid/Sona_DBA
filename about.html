Frequently Asked Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Your Questions, Answered – Find Solutions to All Your Database Concerns.

Do you support cloud-based databases?

Yes, we provide support for databases hosted on all major cloud platforms, including AWS, Azure, Google Cloud, Heroku, and others. We also manage hybrid and on-premise environments.

How does your query approval process work?

For critical operations, we implement a query approval workflow where all important queries go through a review and approval process before execution to maintain data integrity and security.

What happens if my database grows beyond the current capacity?

As your database grows, we proactively recommend and implement upgrades to ensure it continues to operate efficiently, avoiding any performance bottlenecks.

What happens if a critical query is accidentally executed?

We implement a query approval workflow, so any critical or sensitive query needs to be reviewed and approved before execution. This reduces the risk of accidental data loss or changes.

What happens if we lose data? Can it be recovered?

With scheduled backups and our advanced restoration processes, we can quickly recover lost data, minimizing impact on your business.
